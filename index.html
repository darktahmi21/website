Who am I? Interests

Research Interests

I love computers, and I love programming. Pair them together and you see me satisfied when you throw a bunch of problems at me to solve. I'm good at it and I enjoy it. I also love mathematics - which leads me to love machine learning and neural networks, which are my current research interests. I am also studying computer architecture to get good at it.

Machine Learning

I'm currently working on supervised learning and I'm specially interested in regression based output values. There was a problem I had to solve via brute force method in my second year, which I want to create an equation for (weighted equation, of course) and solve the problem at one go.

Comp. Archiecture

I have always been interested in learning how processors worked. I have had courses on this, which I passed with full grades. I am specially interested in GPU computing with SIMT architectures, mainly parallel computing you might want to say. I am currently learning OpenCL because my GTX 560Ti died a few months ago.

Semiconductors

I have worked on multijunction solar cells in my bachelor's thesis and developed a deep interest in semiconductor physics and photonics. My triple junction cells reached about 45% efficiency values where i modeled around 27 different cells.

MEMS

I am currently working on different piezoresistive materials. I am measuring resistances, current density changes and deflection in different shape of piezoresistors. I am hoping to be able to predict a good model (with a good material) that is accurate and reliable.
